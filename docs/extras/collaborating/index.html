Collaborating

You can help the blog in many diferent ways, but for now I’ll just list three of them:

  1. Sharing: Spread the word and share this blog with your friends, family or with someone that you think could benefit from this resource.

  2. Feedback: Send me a mail at CodeKommissar@Gmail.com and tell me what you think of the site (what things you like like/dislike, what could be improved, what topics you’d like to see here, etc)

  3. Economic Support: If you’d like to express your support financially, feel free to use my personal PayPal.Me link for a one-time donation or alternatively check The WebDevBlog’s Patreon Page and make a donation from there.
    Every dollar counts :).
